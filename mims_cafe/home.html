Welcome to Mim's Cafe!

At Mim's Cafe we offer a vareity of food from the Middle-East to America. You will definitely enjoy these food on-the-go or right in our cafe. We are located in St. Paul right by the University of Minnesota - Twin Cities, St. Paul campus. Come on by for lunch or dinner by yourself or with your friends and family and enjoy a wonderful time here.

There is also a coffee shop right next door, if you want to grab something to drink or snack on instead.

We offer catering services, so if you want to order your favorite dish for a party, make sure to place your order today! Have a lovely day!
